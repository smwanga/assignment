About Us

Leina Aviation School

Welcome to Leina Aviation School

Leina Aviation School, a pioneer in general aviation and flight training in Kenya. It was established as Leina Flying School in 1989 under the patronage of His Highness Daniel Moi, President of Kenya.

It is the first flight school certified by the KAA General Civil Aviation Authority (GCAA). Subsequently, lAS diversified into different aviation sectors and changed its name to Leina Aviation School LLC from Leina Flying School in the year 2001.
